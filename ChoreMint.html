What problem are you solving?

Parents struggle to teach money habits through chores in cashless homes. Creating chores, verifying completion, keeping things fair between siblings, and tracking balances/goals becomes tedious and time-consuming.

Kids don't "feel" the value of money because task completion rarely connects to earning, saving, or giving. The connection between work and reward is lost in digital transactions.

Why is this problem important?

Early money habits stick. Without a structured, engaging loop (complete ‚Üí earn ‚Üí save/spend/give ‚Üí feedback), kids miss hands-on practice and families face friction and inconsistency. Solving this reduces parent workload and conflict while building lifelong financial literacy.

üí° Core Solution

How does your service solve the problem?

One-liner: Chore ‚Üí Photo proof ‚Üí Parent approves ‚Üí Instant coins ‚Üí Visible savings progress.

Frictionless start: Pick chore templates, link kids, and get going in minutes.

Photo proof + 1-tap approval: Kids upload before/after; parents approve in <1 min.

Instant feedback loop: Coins drop instantly and fill a goal bar (streaks, badges, confetti).

What makes your solution different?

Progress over checkmarks: Before/After + progress % and estimated reward, not just "done."

Parent-first control: AI can assist, but parents make the final call‚Äîtransparent and fair.

Kid-friendly & safe: Big tappable UI, simple copy, light gamification, and clear allowance history.
